Meditation Space

FAQ

Simple answers about accounts, profile data, avatars, and protected meditation access.

What is Meditation Space?

Meditation Space is a calm web app for meditation, relaxation, and mindful breathing. It gives you a soft digital place where you can slow down, listen to peaceful audio, and take a quiet break from your day.

Do I need an account?

Yes. An account is needed to access the meditation page and save your profile data, such as your name and avatar.

Why do I need to sign up?

Registration lets the app recognize you, show your personal profile, display your avatar, and keep your session active when you return.

How is my avatar stored?

Your avatar image is uploaded to Supabase Storage. After upload, the public avatar URL is saved in your user profile metadata so the app can display it on your profile page.

Can I change my profile data?

Yes. You can change your full name and avatar from the profile page. After saving, the updated data is shown in your profile.

Can I use the meditation page without login?

No. The meditation page is protected. If you are not logged in, the app redirects you to the login page.

What should I do if login does not work?

First, check that your email and password are correct. If you do not have an account yet, create one on the registration page and then try logging in again.

Is my data safe?

Authentication is handled through Supabase. Access is controlled with session checks and storage policies, so users should only access their own protected data.

How do I log out?

You can log out from the account dropdown or from the profile page using the Log out button.
